WhaleTalk

WhaleTalk is an application that makes it easier to instantaneously connect with your classmates. By inputing your own school schedule you are put into a group chat with everyone in your class. Users can choose to interact anonymously or not. We encourage students to connect with their classmates to answer questions, form study groups, or discuss lecture topics.
