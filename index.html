Mission

Whale Startups is a new and innovative startup company based at The University of California, Irvine that aims to develop technology to make people's lives easier. We are a passionate group of individuals that have an ambition to infuse our talent into the tech world. We specialize in cross platform applications and bleeding edge technology. Our members are dedicated to bringing you apps that enhance your favorite activities. We seek to create unique products that connect people and ideas from all over the world.

Products

Groovee is a unique app that connects DJs to their audience.

Whale Talk is an application that makes it easier to instantaneously connect with your classmates. By inputing your own school schedule you are put into a group chat with everyone in your class. Users can choose to interact anonymously or not. We encourage students to connect with their classmates to answer questions, form study groups, or discuss lecture topics.
